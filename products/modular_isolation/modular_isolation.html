Modular Insulation System For Light Commercial Vehicles

MODULAR INSULATION PANELS ARE DESIGNED FOR VANS SUCH AS FIAT, FORD, RENAULT, NISSAN, VOLKSWAGEN, HYUNDAI, PEUGEOT, CITROEN, MERCEDES, OPEL, DACIA, ETC., PROVIDING A DECENT QUALITY AND DESIGN

Modular Insulation Panels are designed for vans such as Fiat, Ford, Renault, Nissan, Volkswagen, Hyundai, Peugeot, Citroen, Mercedes, Opel, Dacia, etc., providing a decent quality and design. Panels are unique to each brand van. It makes better use of its interior space, leaving a maximum area for transporting goods, maintaining thermal insulation properties.

Advantages of Modular Insulation Panel:
- Insulation System: New modular insulation panel installing and removing easily even when the panel damages.
- Suitable for food transportation: The Gel – coat surface of Modular Insulated panel is antibacterial and available to transport any kind of product.
- Hygienic Condition: Thanks ton on porous of Modular Insulation panels the inner surface of the vehicle can be cleaned easily. The waste waters are drained directly through the waster water drainage line around the floor.
- Maximum Cargo Volume: Modular insulation panels are produced according to shape of vehicle doors, roof and floor and save the place of interior.
- Strengths and Light Material: Modular insulation panels light because of the chemical properties. It’s light but quite strength.
- Long Life: FRP has resistance against corrosion and chemicals, for that reason it provides a long life insulated panel.
- Easy Insulation: Insulation can be done easily due to modular design of the parts.

Complete Kit for the van consists of side walls, floor, ceiling, back doors, and also side sliding door. Vans can be equipped with a refrigeration unit and heating system. .
