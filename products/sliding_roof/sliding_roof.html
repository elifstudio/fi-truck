SLIDING ROOF SYSTEM - LINER

Rails: Specially alloyed aluminium rails are used in the production to be fit with a length of the truck and thermic processes are applied (length of Rail: max. 13600 mm)
Roof: Assembled Roofsticks are made of Steel Bearings to be used in Sliding Roof Systems. Thermic processes and toughness tests are applied on Nylon 66 folding plates, which are used for combining bars, to be easily demounted with needless in case of interfere is needed for Tarpaulins.
In Roof: LINER Sliding Roof System to be formed on the top of the truck could be turned into Fixed Roof System and Fixed Roof System could be turned into Sliding Roof System either. There will be no changes on the truck or rails during this transaction.
Sliding Curtain: Side Curtain and Sliding Pillars may be operated independently by using Curtain Runner with Steel Bearings and Plastic Coated Sliding Pillar Runners with Steel Bearings.
