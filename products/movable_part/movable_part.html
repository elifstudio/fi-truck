INTERIOR WALL SYSTEMS

DESCRIPTION OF ELEMENTS

  1. Aluminium rails. Thanks to special designed structure helps easily and effortlessly move the interior wall.
  2. Wall lift bar with gas springs. Adjusting mechanism helps easily and quickly modify the lifting force which makes it universal.
  3. Sandwich panel. Thickness from 32mm up to 100mm. System can be supplied with or without the sandwich panel upon the request of customer.
  4. Locking mechanism of Interior Wall System. Simple mechanism which in one – touch implements locking of system.
  5. PVC profile. Resistant to low tepmeratures (till -60 °С). It has high mechanical strength, calculated over 2500 cycles.
