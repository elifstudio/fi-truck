About

Future Innovations Ltd is manufacturer and partner of leading manufacturers of components for refrigerated boxes, trailers, semi – trailers and chassis. Our company is characterized by an export oriented business model which supplies its products internationally. Technical and sales office, where English, French, Russian and Turkish languages are fluently spoken, guarantees all necessary technical and operational support to any customer all over the universe. Truck and trailer body accessories is a separate category of spare parts and components for trucks: trailers, semi-trailers, vans, refrigerated boxes, boxes, carrosseries, shift buses, mobile laboratories and repair shops, tankers, curtainsiders, tarpaulins, container chassis, chassis vehicles, low-beds, commercial vehicles, truck trailer combinations etc. Every company that uses one or another form of commercial vehicles, primarily concerned that the equipment worked operated as safely - without interruption. After all, even a small unexpected failure could result in serious financial losses cause a breakdown in the supply of products and adversely affect the reputation of the whole enterprise. That's why accessories for a particular commercial vehicle, equipped with special vans , should be chosen thoroughly. «Future Innovation Ltd» offers only the highest quality accessories for carrosseries and specialized equipment. Products from our extensive catalog - the optimal solution for your commercial vehicle at affordable prices. Large inventory of accessories and components is a great opportunity to fully equip the vehicle to work perfectly for a long period.
